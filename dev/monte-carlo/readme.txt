Monte-Carlo simulation and estimation

sim_linear.m - simulates linear demand and estimates
sim_NL.m - nested logit demand
sim_RC.m - Random Coefficient logit demand, using MixedLogitDemand2 class
sim_market.m - Both RC & NL market simulation with BLP instruments, now obsolete
SimMarket.m - Simultation class, used in simulation and tests.
sim_nonlin_price.m - Estimate simulated market with nonlin price.
